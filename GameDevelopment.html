Game: Run if you can

Summary

Main idea: This is going to be an an action game in which player have to survive in a jungle. He will only carry a gun with limited bullets. There will be different kind of animals that will try to attact on our player. Total health of our player will be 150. We will use energy boosters to boostup health and bullets. Player will run to save his life from animals. If an tries to attack on the player then he will shoot that animal. This is going to be a 3D game.
